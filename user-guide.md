# User Guide Sample – Getting Started with Workspace Hub

## Overview

This guide helps new users set up an account and create their first project in Workspace Hub.

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

- A valid work email address
- Access to your email inbox for account verification
- Permission to create projects in your workspace

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Open the registration page.
2. Enter your name, work email, and password.
3. Select **Create account**.
4. Open the verification email and confirm your account.

## Step 2: Sign In

1. Go to the sign-in page.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Select **Sign in**.

## Step 3: Create Your First Project

1. From the dashboard, select **New Project**.
2. Enter the project name and description.
3. Select the appropriate visibility settings.
4. Select **Create**.

## Step 4: Invite Team Members

1. Open the project.
2. Select **Team**.
3. Enter a colleague’s email address.
4. Assign a role.
5. Select **Send invite**.

## Troubleshooting

### I did not receive the verification email

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Confirm that the email address was entered correctly.
- Request a new verification email.

### I cannot create a project

- Check whether your account has project creation permissions.
- Contact your workspace administrator.
